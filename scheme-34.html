Stand Up India Scheme

Description: The Stand Up India scheme provides loans to SC/ST and women entrepreneurs for setting up new enterprises. It aims to promote inclusivity in entrepreneurship and job creation.

Benefits

  • Composite loan between ‚Çπ10 lakh and ‚Çπ1 crore.
  • Includes term loan and working capital assistance.
  • Interest subsidy and handholding support from banks.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must be SC/ST or a woman entrepreneur.
  • Age 18 years and above.
  • Business should be a greenfield project (first-time enterprise).
  • Must not be a defaulter with banks.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate (SC/ST) or Gender Proof (for women)
  • Business Project Report
  • Income Proof / Bank Statement
  • PAN Card
  • Residential Proof

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://standupmitra.in.
  2. Register as an applicant and log in.
  3. Fill details of your business plan and select a bank branch.
  4. Upload documents: Aadhaar, caste certificate, project report.
  5. Submit and track loan application online.
