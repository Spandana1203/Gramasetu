Soil Health Card Scheme ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: The scheme assesses soil nutrient status and issues personalized soil health cards to farmers, guiding scientific fertilizer use for better crop yield and sustainable agriculture.

Eligibility

  • All farmers in Karnataka (small, marginal, and large).
  • Priority for free soil testing to small and marginal farmers.
  • Farmers with cultivable land eligible for soil sample collection and testing.

Benefits

  • Free soil testing and issuance of soil health cards.
  • Crop-wise fertilizer and nutrient guidance.
  • Improved crop yield through scientific nutrient management.
  • Reduced cost of cultivation by avoiding excess fertilizer use.
  • Encourages sustainable and eco-friendly farming practices.

How to Apply

  • Visit nearest Raitha Samparka Kendra (RSK) or Agriculture Department office.
  • Register with Aadhaar, land details (RTC/Pahani), and contact number.
  • Agriculture staff collect soil samples for testing.
  • Test results analyzed in soil testing labs.
  • Soil Health Card issued in print or digital format.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Department of Agriculture & Cooperation, Govt. of India, monitored in Karnataka by Dept. of Agriculture.
  • Cards valid for 2 years, updated after fresh testing.
  • Includes recommendations for primary (N, P, K), secondary, and micronutrients.
  • Farmers trained to interpret cards and use fertilizers accordingly.

Importance

Soil Health Card Scheme enables scientific soil management, enhances crop productivity, reduces costs, and supports sustainable agriculture and environmental protection.
