Unnati Scheme

Description: The Unnati Scheme promotes entrepreneurship among SC/ST communities in Karnataka. It provides financial assistance and support to innovative startups, particularly those addressing social challenges.

Benefits

  • Financial Support: Up to ‚Çπ50 lakh in seed funding
  • Focused Investment: ‚Çπ20 crore dedicated to innovative startups
  • Dual Category: SC/ST entrepreneurs with pioneering ideas; startups addressing social challenges
  • Market Access and Capacity Building for startup growth

Eligibility

  • Ownership: At least 70% by SC/ST members (with caste certificate)
  • Business Structure: Private Ltd, LLP, or other recognized entity in Karnataka
  • Startup Age: Technology-based <4 years; Biotechnology-based <7 years
  • Revenue: Annual turnover < ‚Çπ50 crore
  • Workforce: At least 50% qualified workforce within Karnataka
  • Social Impact: Technological innovation must benefit SC community for social-impact applications

Documents Required

  • SC/ST Certificate
  • Startup Registration Documents
  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Pitch Deck
  • Workforce Details (50% within Karnataka)
  • Bank Account Details

How to Apply

  • Visit Official Website: https://karnataka.gov.in/
  • Login with valid email & mobile number to get OTP
  • Fill in the "Startup Funding for SC/ST" application form
  • Upload required documents
  • Review and submit the application
