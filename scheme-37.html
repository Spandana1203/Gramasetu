Mukhyamantri Anila Bhagya Yojane

Description: This scheme provides free LPG connections to poor families to reduce dependence on traditional fuels like firewood. It was introduced by the Karnataka Government to complement the central Ujjwala Yojana.

Benefits

  • Free LPG connection, stove, and initial refill.
  • Financial assistance worth around ‚Çπ1,600‚Äì‚Çπ1,800 per household.
  • Improved health and reduction of indoor air pollution.

Eligibility

  • Families with BPL cards.
  • Households without an existing LPG connection.
  • Resident of Karnataka with Aadhaar linked to ration card.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • BPL Card / Ration Card
  • Residence proof
  • Mobile number

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://sevasindhu.karnataka.gov.in.
  2. Search for Anila Bhagya scheme and click Apply Online.
  3. Fill BPL details, Aadhaar, and family info.
  4. Upload ration card and Aadhaar card.
  5. Submit and track through Seva Sindhu portal.
