Rural Road Connectivity Scheme (PMGSY Karnataka)

Description: Part of the Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana, Karnataka aims to provide all-weather road connectivity to unconnected rural habitations, improving access to education, healthcare, markets, and government services.

Eligibility

  • Rural habitations with population 500+ (250+ in hilly, tribal, and desert areas).
  • Villages not previously connected by all-weather roads.
  • Projects approved by State Rural Roads Development Agency (SRRDA), Karnataka.

Benefits

  • Construction and upgradation of rural roads with all-weather connectivity.
  • Enhanced access to education, healthcare, and employment opportunities.
  • Boost to local trade, agriculture, and rural industries through better market access.
  • Reduction in travel time and transportation costs for villagers.
  • Social and economic integration of remote and backward regions.

How to Apply

  • No individual application required.
  • Gram Panchayats and local bodies propose projects through District Rural Development Agencies (DRDAs).
  • Approved projects uploaded to PMGSY portal.
  • Villagers can suggest road connectivity needs via Gram Sabha meetings.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India, supported by Karnataka Rural Development & Panchayat Raj Department.
  • Environmentally sustainable practices and quality monitoring in road construction.
  • Priority on connecting agricultural hubs, health centers, and schools.
  • Road maintenance ensured through Panchayats and state funds.

Importance

PMGSY Karnataka enhances social, economic, and cultural integration by providing all-weather roads, improving access to agriculture, education, healthcare, and trade.
