Kaushalya Karnataka Skill Training

Description: The Kaushalya Karnataka scheme provides skill development training for unemployed youth in the state. It equips them with job-ready skills and helps them secure employment.

Benefits

  • Free skill development training in over 200 trades.
  • Certification upon course completion.
  • Job placement assistance through government tie-ups.
  • Monthly stipend during training in some categories.

Eligibility

  • Age between 18‚Äì35 years.
  • Resident of Karnataka.
  • Should be unemployed or underemployed.
  • Priority to SC/ST/OBC, minorities, and women candidates.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Age proof (SSLC/PUC certificate)
  • Residence proof
  • Income Certificate (if required)
  • Passport-size photos

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://kaushalkar.com.
  2. Register as a candidate.
  3. Choose a skill course and training center.
  4. Upload documents and attend training sessions.
