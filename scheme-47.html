Karnataka Digital Learning Portals

Description: These are online learning platforms launched by the Karnataka government to provide digital resources for school and college students. It includes e-content, recorded lectures, practice tests, and exam preparation material.

Eligibility

  • Open to all students studying in Karnataka schools and colleges.
  • No income or caste restrictions.

Benefits

  • Free access to digital textbooks, e-learning videos, and practice questions.
  • Coverage of SSLC, PUC, CET, NEET, and higher education materials.
  • Helps bridge the digital divide for rural students.

How to Apply

No application needed; visit https://karnatakaeducation.gov.in and access resources directly.
