Free Coaching Scheme

Description: The Karnataka government provides free coaching facilities for SC/ST/OBC/minority students to prepare for competitive examinations such as IAS, KAS, UPSC, SSC, Banking, and other government jobs.

Benefits

  • Free coaching classes in reputed institutions.
  • Study materials and access to libraries.
  • Hostel and food facilities for selected candidates.
  • Stipend in some categories to support preparation.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must be a resident of Karnataka.
  • Must belong to SC/ST/OBC/minority categories.
  • Should have completed graduation (for higher exams like IAS/KAS).
  • Family income should not exceed ‚Çπ8 lakh per annum (varies by category).

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Marks card of qualifying exam (SSLC/PUC/degree)
  • Caste Certificate (SC/ST/OBC)
  • Income Certificate
  • Passport-size photos

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://swdservices.karnataka.gov.in/petccoaching.
  2. Register as a student.
  3. Upload caste certificate, marks card, and Aadhaar.
  4. Submit and wait for selection.
