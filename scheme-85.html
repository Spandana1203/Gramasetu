Amrith Gram Panchayat Development Scheme ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: Rural development initiative in Karnataka to improve basic infrastructure, sanitation, education, and livelihood opportunities by empowering Gram Panchayats to plan and implement projects based on local needs.

Eligibility

  • All Gram Panchayats across Karnataka.
  • Priority to backward, drought-prone, and underdeveloped villages.
  • Projects must align with state rural development guidelines.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance to Gram Panchayats for development projects.
  • Improvement of rural infrastructure: roads, drinking water, street lighting, sanitation.
  • Strengthening of schools, Anganwadis, and healthcare facilities.
  • Support for livelihood programs, women empowerment, and farmer welfare.
  • Encourages community participation in decision-making and planning.

How to Apply

  • Gram Panchayat members prepare a Development Action Plan (DAP) based on local needs.
  • Submit the proposal to Taluk Panchayat or District Panchayat Office.
  • Approved projects receive funding and are monitored by the Rural Development & Panchayat Raj Department, Karnataka.
  • Villagers can participate in Gram Sabha meetings to suggest priorities.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by the Rural Development and Panchayat Raj Department, Government of Karnataka.
  • Funds used for time-bound, need-based village projects.
  • Monitoring and evaluation through online portals for transparency.
  • Promotes self-reliant villages by strengthening local governance.
