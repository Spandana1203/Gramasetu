Yuva Nidhi Scheme (Unemployment Support)

Description: Launched on January 12, 2024, the Yuva Nidhi Scheme provides financial assistance to unemployed graduates and diploma holders in Karnataka. It is part of the state government's Five Guarantee Schemes, helping youth financially while they search for jobs.

Benefits

  • Graduates: ‚Çπ3,000 per month
  • Diploma holders: ‚Çπ1,500 per month
  • Duration: Up to 2 years or until employment is secured (whichever is earlier)
  • Mode: Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to Aadhaar-linked bank account

Eligibility

  • Must be a resident of Karnataka for at least 6 years.
  • Must have completed graduation or diploma in the academic year 2022‚Äì23.
  • Must be unemployed for at least 6 months after completing studies.
  • Not eligible if employed, self-employed, pursuing higher studies, or enrolled in an apprenticeship.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Educational Certificates (SSLC, PUC, Degree/Diploma)
  • Bank Account Details (Aadhaar-linked)
  • Recent Passport-size Photograph
  • Income Certificate (if applicable)

How to Apply (Steps)

  • Register on Seva Sindhu Portal.
  • Enter your degree/diploma registration number.
  • Upload required documents.
  • Submit self-declaration every 3 months confirming unemployment status.
  • Receive monthly allowance directly into your bank account.
