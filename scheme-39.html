Raita Belaku Scheme

Description: This scheme provides income support to small and medium farmers in Karnataka by directly transferring benefits into their accounts. Unlike loan waivers, it focuses on providing consistent financial assistance.

Benefits

  • Annual financial assistance of around ‚Çπ5,000 per farmer family.
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to bank accounts.
  • Support for meeting input costs and household expenses.

Eligibility

  • Farmers who own cultivable land (small and medium landholders).
  • Must have Farmer ID issued by Agriculture Department.
  • Excludes large farmers and institutional landholders.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Farmer ID (from Agriculture Department)
  • RTC (land ownership record)
  • Bank details
  • Residence proof

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://sevasindhu.karnataka.gov.in.
  2. Log in using Aadhaar and mobile number.
  3. Fill details of land ownership and farmer details.
  4. Upload land records and Aadhaar.
  5. Submit and wait for Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT).
