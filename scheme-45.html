Udyoga Mitra

Description: Karnataka Udyoga Mitra provides a platform for entrepreneurs to set up industries by offering clearances, approvals, and incentives under a single-window system.

Benefits

  • Assistance in obtaining approvals and licenses.
  • Subsidies on land, electricity, and taxes depending on project size.
  • Handholding support for start-ups and investors.
  • Online application tracking and grievance redressal.

Eligibility

  • Any entrepreneur or company looking to establish a new business in Karnataka.
  • Must comply with government norms and submit a project report.
  • Special benefits for SC/ST, women, and MSME entrepreneurs.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Project report (for business applicants)
  • Bank account details
  • Address proof

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://ebiz.karnataka.gov.in.
  2. Create an account and log in.
  3. Fill Single Window Clearance form for business.
  4. Upload documents and submit.
