Swavalambi Sarathi Scheme (Self-Employment Initiative)

Description: Eligible beneficiaries receive 50% subsidy or up to ‚Çπ3,00,000 for purchasing vehicles such as passenger auto rickshaws, goods vehicles, and taxis.

Benefits

  • Subsidy: 50% of vehicle cost or up to ‚Çπ3,00,000.
  • Loan Facility: Remaining amount can be availed as a bank loan.
  • Priority to Women applicants.
  • Asset Ownership: Beneficiaries gain ownership of the vehicle for self-employment.

Eligibility

  • Belong to a recognized religious minority community.
  • Permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Age between 18 and 55 years.
  • Annual family income not exceeding ‚Çπ6,00,000.
  • No family member should be employed in State/Central Government or PSU.
  • Possess valid driving license issued by Regional Transport Authority.
  • Should not have availed any vehicle loan under State Government schemes previously.
  • Not a KMDC loan defaulter.

How to Apply

  • Online: Visit KMDC Portal, select "Swavalambi Sarathi Scheme", fill personal details, upload documents, and submit.
  • Offline: Collect application from District Agriculture Office or KMDC District Office, fill form, attach documents, and submit to the same office.
  • Ensure all documents are self-attested.

Documents Required

  • Caste Certificate.
  • Income Certificate.
  • Aadhaar Card.
  • Valid Driving License.
  • Vehicle Quotation/Proforma Invoice from dealer.
  • Bank Passbook Copy.
  • Self-Declaration Form confirming authenticity and non-availment of previous vehicle loans.
