Free Laptop Scheme for SC/ST Students

Description: This scheme provides free laptops to meritorious SC/ST students pursuing higher education in Karnataka. It promotes digital inclusion and ensures equal access to technology for marginalized groups.

Benefits

  • Free laptop worth ‚Çπ25,000 ‚Äì ‚Çπ35,000.
  • Access to e-learning, online resources, and digital tools.
  • Encourages use of technology in higher studies.

Eligibility

  • Student must belong to SC/ST category.
  • Must be enrolled in professional/technical courses such as engineering, medicine, law, or degree courses.
  • Priority given to students from government and aided institutions.
  • Family annual income should be within prescribed limits (usually below ‚Çπ2.5 lakh).

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate (SC/ST)
  • Admission proof (college ID or fee receipt)
  • Marks card of qualifying exam
  • Passport-size photo

How to Apply

  1. Check notifications on https://dce.karnataka.gov.in.
  2. Apply through your college by submitting the application with supporting documents.
  3. Attach Aadhaar, caste certificate, and admission proof.
  4. College forwards application to the department.
