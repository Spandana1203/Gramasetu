Raitha Siri Scheme (Millet Farmers‚Äô Support)

Description: Karnataka Government initiative encouraging cultivation of millets (ragi, jowar, bajra) for higher income and sustainable agriculture. Supports climate-smart farming, food security, and revival of traditional crops.

Eligibility

  • Farmer and permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Cultivation of millet crops (ragi, jowar, bajra) on own farmland.
  • Land ownership proof (RTC/land records).
  • Priority to small/marginal farmers, SC/ST, women.
  • Valid Aadhaar-linked bank account for DBT transfers.

Benefits

  • ‚Çπ10,000 per hectare financial assistance.
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to Aadhaar-linked bank account.
  • Encourages cultivation of ragi, jowar, bajra, and other millets.
  • Promotes climate-resilient sustainable farming practices.
  • Enhances farmer income via crop diversification.
  • Supports nutrition security for communities.
  • Improves soil fertility and reduces water dependency.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card (linked with bank account).
  • RTC / land ownership document.
  • Residence proof (ration card, voter ID, etc.).
  • Bank passbook copy (Aadhaar-seeded).
  • Caste & income certificate (if applicable).
  • Passport-size photographs.
  • Application form/self-declaration of millet cultivation.

How to Apply

  1. Visit nearest Raitha Samparka Kendra (RSK) or Agriculture Department office.
  2. Collect and fill out Raitha Siri Scheme application form.
  3. Attach land records, Aadhaar, and bank details.
  4. Submit form to Agriculture Officer at RSK.
  5. Verification through land and crop records.
  6. Approved farmers receive DBT financial assistance.
  7. Apply online via Seva Sindhu Portal.
  8. Track application status locally or on the portal.

Importance

  • Revives Karnataka‚Äôs millet culture.
  • Boosts farmer income through millet cultivation.
  • Promotes climate-resilient crops in drought-prone areas.
  • Strengthens nutrition security with traditional grains.
  • Encourages sustainable farming with less water usage.
  • Supports Karnataka‚Äôs vision as ‚ÄúMillet Capital of India.‚Äù
