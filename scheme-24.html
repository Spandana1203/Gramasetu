Ganga Kalyana Scheme (Water & Irrigation)

Description: Financial assistance for construction of irrigation infrastructure including individual borewells/open wells for farmers with 2‚Äì5 acres land and lift irrigation systems. Administered by Karnataka Minority Development Corporation (KMDC).

Benefits

  • Subsidized Financial Assistance: Up to ‚Çπ1.5 lakh per beneficiary including pump sets and electrification.
  • Improved Irrigation: Reduces dependence on erratic rainfall.
  • Enhanced Agricultural Productivity: Enables multiple cropping cycles.
  • Empowerment of Minority Farmers: Supports socio-economic development.

Eligibility

  • Belong to a Minority Community recognized by Karnataka Government.
  • Permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Small or marginal farmer owning 2‚Äì5 acres of land.
  • Annual family income not exceeding ‚Çπ22,000.
  • Applicants must be at least 18 years old. (Maximum age limit of 55 years removed)

How to Apply

  • Online: Visit KMDC Portal, select "Ganga Kalyana Scheme", fill personal details, upload required documents, and submit.
  • Offline: Obtain application from District Agriculture Office or KMDC District Office, fill details, attach documents, and submit to the same office.
  • Ensure all documents are self-attested.

Documents Required

  • Caste Certificate issued by competent authority.
  • Income Certificate for annual family income verification.
  • Aadhaar Card for identity and residence proof.
  • Latest RTC (Record of Rights, Tenancy, and Crops) to confirm land ownership.
  • Small/Very Small Tenant Certificate, if applicable.
  • Bank Passbook Copy for subsidy/loan disbursement.
  • Receipt of Land Revenue payment.
  • Self-Declaration Letter confirming authenticity of information.
  • Self-Declaration Letter of Guarantor, if applicable.
