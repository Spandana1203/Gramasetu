National Means-cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMS)

Description: NMMS is a central government scholarship scheme implemented in Karnataka for students from economically weaker families. It provides financial support to meritorious students studying in class 9 to 12.

Benefits

  • ‚Çπ1,000 per month for four years (class 9 to 12).
  • Paid directly into student‚Äôs bank account.
  • Encourages students to continue secondary education.

Eligibility

  • Student must be studying in class 8 to appear for selection test.
  • Family annual income should not exceed ‚Çπ3.5 lakh.
  • Must secure at least 55% in previous class (50% for SC/ST).

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Marks card of previous class
  • Income Certificate (family income below ‚Çπ3.5 lakh)
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photograph

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://scholarships.gov.in.
  2. Register as a student on the NSP portal.
  3. Fill details and upload documents: Income certificate, Aadhaar, marks card.
  4. Submit application for NMMS scholarship.
