PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: Central Government income support scheme providing direct financial assistance to farmers in Karnataka to meet agricultural and household needs.

Eligibility

  • All small and marginal farmers owning cultivable land.
  • Eligible families: husband, wife, and minor children.
  • Exclusions: Institutional landholders, government employees, income-tax payers, professionals (doctors, engineers, lawyers).
  • Must have valid Aadhaar and land ownership records.

Benefits

  • Direct income support of ‚Çπ6,000/year, paid in three installments of ‚Çπ2,000 each.
  • Amount credited directly via DBT to farmers‚Äô bank accounts.
  • Helps cover cultivation costs, seeds, fertilizers, and household expenses.
  • Transparent, Aadhaar-linked, corruption-free process.
  • Supports millions of farmers nationwide, including Karnataka.

How to Apply

  • Visit the official PM-KISAN portal: pmkisan.gov.in
  • Apply through nearest Raitha Samparka Kendra (RSK) or Common Service Centre (CSC).
  • Register with Aadhaar, land documents (RTC/Pahani), and bank account details.
  • Application verified by local Agriculture Department.
  • Check beneficiary status and installment details online.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Government of India.
  • Karnataka Agriculture Department facilitates registration and verification.
  • Regular SMS updates on installment release.
  • Grievance redressal and helpline available on PM-KISAN portal.

Importance

PM-KISAN ensures timely financial support to farmers, stabilizes income, helps manage cultivation costs, and promotes financial security in agriculture.
