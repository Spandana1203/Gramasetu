Udyogini Scheme

Description: The Udyogini Scheme promotes self-reliance among women by providing financial assistance for setting up small-scale businesses. Interest-free loans are provided to widows, disabled, and destitute women, along with skill development and support across 88 business categories.

Benefits

  • Loans up to ‚Çπ3 lakh for small businesses
  • Interest-free loans for special categories
  • Skill development through training programs
  • Support for diverse business activities: retail, manufacturing, services (88 categories)

Eligibility

  • Age: 18‚Äì55 years
  • Family annual income below ‚Çπ1.5 lakh
  • Permanent resident of Karnataka
  • Engaged in small-scale business/self-employment
  • No default on previous loans

Documents Required

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar, Voter ID, Ration Card)
  • Age Proof (Birth Certificate/School Leaving Certificate)
  • Income Proof (Family Income Certificate)
  • Address Proof (Utility bill/Rental agreement)
  • Project Report for proposed business
  • Bank Account Details
  • Passport-sized Photographs

How to Apply

  • Online: Visit KSWDC Official Website, download & fill form, attach documents, submit to nearest bank or KSWDC office
  • Offline: Visit local bank, collect form, fill & attach documents, submit to bank official
