Arogyavani Health Helpline (Dial 104)

Description: The Arogyavani Helpline (104) is a toll-free health helpline launched by the Government of Karnataka to provide 24/7 medical advice, counseling, and health-related information to citizens over the phone.

Benefits

  • Free medical advice from trained doctors and counselors
  • Information on diseases, prevention, and treatment
  • Counseling for mental health and stress management
  • Guidance on maternal and child health services
  • Support during medical emergencies (linked to ambulance services like 108)

Eligibility

  • All residents of Karnataka can use the service
  • No age, income, or category restrictions

How to Use

  • Dial 104 (toll-free number) from any phone
  • Speak to a health counselor or doctor
  • Receive advice, counseling, or referral to nearby hospitals
