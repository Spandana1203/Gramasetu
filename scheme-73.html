Kannada Language Learning for Rural Youth

Description: Initiative to promote Kannada literacy among rural youth by providing training in reading, writing, and speaking. Integrates digital platforms and skill development to improve communication, education access, and employability.

Eligibility

  • Rural youth aged 15‚Äì30 years.
  • Preference to students, unemployed youth, and self-help group (SHG) members.
  • No prior formal education required.

Benefits

  • Free/low-cost Kannada classes at community centers, schools, and Panchayats.
  • Digital learning modules through Karnataka Digital Learning Portals.
  • Improved access to government schemes, jobs, and higher education.
  • Development of communication skills and self-confidence for exams, interviews, and business.
  • Support to preserve and promote Kannada culture.

How to Apply

  1. Visit your Gram Panchayat or Taluk Education Office to register.
  2. Apply online via Seva Sindhu Portal under education/skill development schemes.
  3. Choose Kannada language training program (offline or digital).
  4. Upload Aadhaar, age proof, and residence certificate.
  5. Receive confirmation via SMS/Email.

Additional Details

  • Classes conducted by trained Kannada teachers and volunteers.
  • Learning materials available in print, audio, and mobile app formats.
  • Linked with Kaushalya Karnataka Skill Training and Karnataka Digital Learning Portals for career-oriented learning.
  • Certificates provided upon completion to help in employment opportunities.

Importance

Enhances Kannada literacy in rural youth while supporting employment, education, and cultural knowledge.
