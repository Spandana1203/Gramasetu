National Handloom Development Programme (NHDP)

Description: The NHDP is aimed at improving the handloom sector by providing weavers with financial assistance, infrastructure support, and marketing opportunities. It helps traditional artisans sustain their livelihood while modernizing weaving practices.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for raw materials and upgraded looms.
  • Training in modern weaving techniques.
  • Access to government exhibitions and e-commerce marketing.
  • Subsidies on equipment and raw material procurement.

Eligibility

  • Must be a registered handloom weaver.
  • Must hold a Weaver ID card issued by the Handloom Department.
  • Priority given to economically weaker weavers and women artisans.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Weaver ID / Handloom Registration Certificate
  • Caste & Income Certificate (if applicable)
  • Bank Passbook Copy
  • Residential Proof
  • Passport-size Photograph

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://handlooms.nic.in.
  2. Select the scheme and download the application form.
  3. Fill details and attach documents: Weaver ID, Aadhaar, income certificate.
  4. Submit through the local handloom office or cooperative society.
  5. Receive approval and assistance after verification.
