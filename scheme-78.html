Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY) ‚Äì Organic Farming Karnataka

Description: PKVY promotes cluster-based organic farming, encouraging farmers to reduce chemical inputs, adopt eco-friendly methods, and market organic produce collectively.

Eligibility

  • Farmers or farmer groups in Karnataka willing to adopt organic farming.
  • Priority for small and marginal farmers.
  • Clusters formed at village level (minimum 20 farmers, 50 acres) for collective farming.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for organic farming practices.
  • Support for bio-fertilizers, organic manure, and eco-friendly pest management.
  • Formation of farmer clusters.
  • Certification support under Participatory Guarantee System (PGS).
  • Improved soil fertility, reduced input costs, and better market prices for organic crops.

How to Apply

  • Visit official PKVY portal: https://pgsindia-ncof.gov.in.
  • Or visit nearest Raitha Samparka Kendra (RSK) / Agriculture Department office.
  • Register as farmer or farmer group with Aadhaar, land documents, and bank details.
  • Submit application to join an organic farming cluster.
  • Receive training, input support, and certification guidance through Agriculture Department.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Govt. of India, with support from Karnataka Agriculture Department.
  • Each cluster receives financial assistance of up to ‚Çπ10 lakh for three years.
  • Focus on production, certification, and marketing of organic produce.
  • Farmers linked with organic markets, exhibitions, and e-platforms for better income.

Importance

PKVY provides low-cost, eco-friendly, and profitable organic farming experience. It improves soil health, yields, market prices, and promotes sustainable agriculture with collective farmer knowledge exchange.
