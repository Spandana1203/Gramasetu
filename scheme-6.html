Vajpayee Arogyasri Yojana

Description: Launched in 2009, the Vajpayee Arogyashree Yojana provides free tertiary healthcare to economically vulnerable families in Karnataka. It covers high-cost medical treatments at empaneled hospitals across the state.

Benefits

  • Annual Coverage: ‚Çπ1.5 lakh per family (floater basis)
  • Additional Buffer: ‚Çπ50,000 per year on a case-by-case basis
  • Treatment Coverage: 402 procedures including cardiovascular, cancer, neurological, renal, burns, polytrauma, and neonatal surgeries
  • Cashless Treatment: Available at empaneled government and private hospitals
  • No Premium: Fully funded by the state government
  • Follow-up Care: Up to 50 follow-up procedures covered for one year

Eligibility

  • Must be a resident of Karnataka
  • Must hold a valid BPL ration card issued by the Karnataka government
  • No age limit for beneficiaries
  • Up to 5 family members per year can avail benefits

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • BPL Ration Card
  • PDS Card
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Bank Account Details (for claim settlements, if applicable)

How to Apply (Steps)

  • Visit an empaneled hospital or health center participating in the Arogya Karnataka Scheme: Arogya Karnataka
  • Provide Aadhaar card, BPL ration card, and PDS card to enrollment staff
  • Undergo biometric authentication with CIDR Aadhaar Server
  • Complete e-KYC process (details auto-populated)
  • Receive unique Arogya Karnataka ID (ArKID)
  • Use ArKID to access cashless treatment at empaneled hospitals
