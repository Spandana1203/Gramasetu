Gruha Lakshmi Scheme

Description: This scheme provides ‚Çπ2,000 per month to the woman head of the family (as per ration card) to support household expenses and make women financially independent.

Benefits

  • ‚Çπ2,000 monthly assistance directly transferred to the woman‚Äôs Aadhaar-linked bank account through DBT.
  • Helps meet essential needs like healthcare, education, and improves financial stability.

Eligibility

  • Woman must be the head of the family with BPL/APL/Antyodaya ration card.
  • Annual income must be below ‚Çπ2 lakh.
  • Taxpayers, GST payers, government employees, or beneficiaries of similar schemes are not eligible.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card of the applicant (and spouse, if applicable)
  • Ration card (BPL, APL, or Antyodaya)
  • Bank account details (Aadhaar-linked)
  • Income certificate
  • Residence proof, caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Mobile number, passport-size photograph, etc.

How to Apply (Online ‚Äì Seva Sindhu Portal)

  • Visit Seva Sindhu Portal.
  • Register (if new user) or log in (if already registered).
  • Click ‚ÄúApply for Services‚Äù ‚Üí Select ‚ÄúGruha Lakshmi Scheme‚Äù.
  • Fill in personal, contact, and bank details.
  • Upload required documents, agree to self-declaration, and complete Aadhaar verification via OTP.
  • Submit the form and note your application number for future reference.
  • To check status, log in and go to ‚ÄúGruhalakshmi Application Status‚Äù.
