Niranthara Jyothi Yojane (Agricultural Electrification Subsidy)

Description: A flagship program by the Government of Karnataka to provide 24-hour uninterrupted power to rural areas. Feeder bifurcation ensures reliable electricity for agricultural and non-agricultural use. Implemented in two phases across 129 taluks at a total cost of ‚Çπ2,123 crores (40% government funding, 60% ESCOM loans).

Benefits

  • 24/7 Power Supply: Continuous electricity to rural households and farms.
  • Reduced Load-Shedding: Minimized power interruptions.
  • Improved Agricultural Productivity: Reliable power for irrigation and farming activities.
  • Enhanced Consumer Satisfaction: More hours of supply and better service quality.
  • Economic Growth: Supports small and household enterprises in rural areas.

Eligibility

  • Farmers requiring consistent power for irrigation and agriculture.
  • Rural households benefiting from improved electricity supply.
  • Electricity Supply Companies (ESCOMs) responsible for implementation and management.

Documents Required (for Farmers)

  • Aadhaar Card ‚Äì Identity verification
  • Land Ownership Documents ‚Äì Proof of agricultural land ownership
  • Electricity Bill ‚Äì Verify existing connection and usage
  • Bank Account Details ‚Äì For subsidy or reimbursement (if applicable)

How to Apply

  • Contact Local ESCOM Office: Visit nearest ESCOM office or official website.
  • Submit Required Documents: Land proof, electricity bill, Aadhaar card.
  • Follow ESCOM Guidelines: Adhere to local ESCOM procedures.
