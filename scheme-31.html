Sabka Vikas Panchayat Development Plan (GPDP)

Description: The GPDP is part of a nationwide initiative where Gram Panchayats prepare annual development plans that reflect local needs. This scheme ensures that people at the grassroots level directly participate in deciding developmental priorities.

Benefits

  • Community-driven development works (roads, water supply, schools, sanitation).
  • Funds allocated under central and state schemes are distributed based on local needs.
  • Greater transparency and accountability through participatory governance.

Eligibility

  • All rural residents are eligible to participate.
  • Any registered voter in the Gram Sabha can give suggestions.
  • Beneficiaries of selected development works are prioritized based on income, caste, and local need.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Residential Proof (village certificate)
  • Suggestion/Application form filled during Gram Sabha

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://gpdp.nic.in.
  2. Participate in Gram Sabha meetings where development plans are discussed.
  3. Provide your suggestions for welfare and development needs.
  4. Ensure your name and requirements are included in the Panchayat Development Plan.
  5. Approved projects are implemented and monitored online.
