Amrith Farmer Producer Organization (FPO) Subsidy Scheme ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: State-level initiative to support the formation and strengthening of Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) by providing financial aid, capacity-building, and market linkages for better production, processing, and marketing of agricultural produce.

Eligibility

  • Groups of 10‚Äì15 farmers willing to form an FPO.
  • Priority to small/marginal farmers, women farmers, and SC/ST groups.
  • FPO must be registered under Companies Act (Producer Company) or Cooperative Societies Act.
  • Farmers must have cultivable land and valid land records (RTC/Pahani).

Benefits

  • Financial subsidy for establishing and running FPOs in rural areas.
  • Support for infrastructure: storage, grading, packaging, processing units.
  • Training programs on governance, accounting, and agribusiness management.
  • Assistance in accessing institutional credit and government schemes.
  • Market linkages with buyers, food processing units, and e-NAM platforms.
  • Improved bargaining power and better price realization for farmers.

How to Apply

  • Form a group and register as an FPO via Registrar of Companies or Cooperative Registrar.
  • Apply through Department of Agriculture, Karnataka or NABARD facilitation centers.
  • Submit documents: Aadhaar, land ownership proof, bank details, FPO registration certificate.
  • Application reviewed and subsidy released to FPO account.
  • Continuous monitoring and support provided by Agriculture Department and NABARD.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Department of Agriculture, Karnataka with support from NABARD and Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, India.
  • Subsidy up to 35% of project cost for infrastructure.
  • Special focus on millet, horticulture, dairy, and allied sectors.
  • Encourages collective farming, value addition, and rural entrepreneurship.
