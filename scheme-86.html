Amrith Rural Housing Scheme ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: State initiative aimed at providing affordable housing to rural families, especially economically weaker sections, ensuring safe and permanent houses with basic amenities.

Eligibility

  • Resident of rural Karnataka.
  • Belongs to BPL or EWS category.
  • Should not already own a pucca house under any other housing scheme.
  • Preference for landless, widowed, and disabled applicants.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for new house construction.
  • Subsidy support to upgrade kutcha houses into pucca houses.
  • Priority to SC/ST, widows, disabled persons, and BPL households.
  • Access to sanitation, electricity, and drinking water facilities.
  • Promotes rural development and reduces homelessness.

How to Apply

  • Visit Seva Sindhu portal: https://sevasindhu.karnataka.gov.in
  • Register with Aadhaar, ration card, income certificate, and land/residence details.
  • Fill out the Amrith Rural Housing Scheme application.
  • Upload required documents: Aadhaar, BPL card, caste certificate (if applicable), land proof.
  • Submit application; verification by Gram Panchayat and Taluk Panchayat Office.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Rural Development & Panchayat Raj Department, Karnataka.
  • Houses constructed using government subsidy and beneficiary contribution.
  • Funds transferred directly via DBT.
  • Aligned with PMAY-Gramin for maximum coverage.
