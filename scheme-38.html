Karmika Sahaya Hasta Scheme

Description: The scheme provides welfare benefits to unorganized sector workers registered under the Karnataka Labour Department. It ensures financial support for social security and emergencies.

Benefits

  • Financial aid during hospitalization.
  • Maternity benefit for women workers.
  • Educational scholarships for children of workers.
  • Accidental and natural death compensation.

Eligibility

  • Must be a registered unorganized sector worker.
  • Age between 18‚Äì60 years.
  • Must possess Labour Card issued by Karnataka government.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Labour ID Card / Employment proof
  • Bank account details
  • Income Certificate
  • Passport-size photo

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://labour.karnataka.gov.in.
  2. Register as a worker in the unorganized sector.
  3. Apply for benefits under Sahaya Hasta scheme.
  4. Upload documents: Labour ID, Aadhaar, bank details.
  5. Submit and track through Labour portal.
