Anna Bhagya Scheme (Free Rice Distribution)

Description: The Anna Bhagya Scheme is a food security program by the Government of Karnataka. It provides free rice every month to poor families holding Below Poverty Line (BPL) or Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) ration cards.

Benefits

  • Each eligible person receives 10 kg of free rice per month.
  • Helps reduce hunger and ensures food security for vulnerable households.
  • Direct cash transfer is provided if rice distribution faces supply issues.

Eligibility

  • Must be a resident of Karnataka.
  • Must hold a BPL card or Antyodaya Anna Yojana (AAY) ration card.
  • Priority given to economically weaker sections, SC/ST, and vulnerable groups.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • BPL or AAY Ration Card
  • Proof of residence (if not updated in ration card)
  • Passport-size photograph

How to Apply (Steps)

  • Visit your nearest ration shop (Fair Price Shop) or Food & Civil Supplies office.
  • Submit your BPL/AAY ration card and Aadhaar details.
  • Officials will verify eligibility through ration records.
  • Once approved, rice will be distributed monthly at the ration shop.
