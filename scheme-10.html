Sanjeevini ‚Äì NRLM

Description: Sanjeevini is a Karnataka state initiative under the National Rural Livelihoods Mission (NRLM). It focuses on enhancing livelihood opportunities and income security for rural poor households, especially women, and aims to empower rural communities through self-help groups (SHGs), skill development, and micro-enterprise support.

Benefits

  • Formation of Self-Help Groups (SHGs) for collective income generation
  • Financial assistance and loans for small businesses or entrepreneurial activities
  • Skill development programs to enhance employability
  • Capacity building for rural women and marginalized communities
  • Support for micro-enterprises and market linkages

Eligibility

  • Rural households in Karnataka, especially women-headed households
  • Below Poverty Line (BPL) families or households in vulnerable socio-economic conditions
  • Willingness to participate in SHG formation and skill development programs

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card (identity proof)
  • Proof of residence (ration card, voter ID)
  • BPL certificate (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photograph

How to Apply

  • Visit the Block/Gram Panchayat office where the NRLM/Sanjeevini program is implemented
  • Express interest in joining the program or forming a Self-Help Group
  • Submit required documents (proof of residence, Aadhaar, BPL certificate if available)
  • Participate in training and skill development programs
  • Access financial support or micro-credit once approved by the program authorities
