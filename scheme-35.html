Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana (PMJDY)

Description: PMJDY is a financial inclusion program that provides banking services to every household. It ensures that even rural families have access to savings accounts, insurance, and direct benefit transfers.

Benefits

  • Zero-balance savings account.
  • Free RuPay debit card with accident insurance cover of ‚Çπ1 lakh.
  • Overdraft facility up to ‚Çπ10,000 after six months.
  • Direct credit of government subsidies into the account.

Eligibility

  • Any Indian citizen without a bank account.
  • Priority to rural and low-income families.
  • Minors above 10 years can open accounts with guardians.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card / Voter ID / PAN
  • Address Proof (Ration Card, Driving License, etc.)
  • 2 Passport-size Photographs

How to Apply

  1. Visit your nearest bank branch.
  2. Fill the PMJDY account opening form.
  3. Submit Aadhaar, passport-size photo, and address proof.
  4. Get the account opened with zero balance and receive a RuPay card.
