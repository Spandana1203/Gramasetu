Karnataka Panchayat Development Scheme

Description: This scheme, launched by the RDPR Department, assigns senior officials‚Äîfrom Panchayat Development Officers to Additional Chief Secretaries‚Äîto adopt Gram Panchayats. Adopted GPs receive customized support to elevate governance, infrastructure, and implementation of rural development schemes.

Benefits

  • Better governance and accountability in Gram Panchayats
  • Improved tax collection for more local development funds
  • Stronger implementation of rural schemes like MGNREGA
  • Infrastructure upgrades: roads, schools, water, and health facilities
  • Use of digital tools for transparency and monitoring

Eligibility

  • All Gram Panchayats in Karnataka (~6,000)
  • Applies at Panchayat level; not for individuals

Documents Required

  • No documents required from citizens
  • Aadhaar, voter ID, or ration card may be used when accessing related services

How to Apply

This is not a citizen-facing scheme; no application is required. Implementation is done by government officials. Citizens can:

  • Visit local Gram Panchayat or Block Development Office (BDO)
  • Request information about development initiatives under the scheme
  • Check GP progress via Panchatantra (official GP tracking platform)
