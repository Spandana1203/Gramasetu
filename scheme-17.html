KBOCWWB Pension Scheme

Description: The KBOCWWB Pension Scheme is a welfare initiative by the Government of Karnataka providing financial security to registered building and construction workers aged 60+. Workers receive monthly pensions and social welfare support.

Benefits

  • Monthly pension up to ‚Çπ2,000
  • Financial security for retired construction workers
  • Part of broader social welfare: medical assistance, accident benefits, educational support for children

Eligibility

  • Minimum age: 60 years
  • Registered with KBOCWWB
  • Paid subscription fees for at least 3 consecutive years
  • Not receiving similar pension from another government scheme

How to Apply

  • Visit Seva Sindhu Portal: https://sevasindhu.karnataka.gov.in/
  • Login with Email/Mobile and Password
  • Navigate to Services ‚Üí Karnataka Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board ‚Üí Pension Scheme
  • Fill application form accurately
  • Upload required documents
  • Submit application

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Age Proof (Birth Certificate or School Leaving Certificate)
  • Employment Certificate (proof of construction work)
  • Bank Account Details
  • Recent Passport-sized Photograph
