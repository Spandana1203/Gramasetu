Solar Irrigation Pumpsets Scheme

Description: A scheme to provide sustainable and cost-effective irrigation solutions for farmers by installing solar-powered pumpsets, promoting renewable energy in agriculture and reducing dependence on conventional power.

Benefits

  • Cost Savings: Reduces electricity bills using solar energy.
  • Sustainability: Encourages renewable energy in agriculture.
  • Reliability: Ensures continuous water supply for irrigation, even in remote areas.
  • Environmentally Friendly: Reduces carbon footprint by lowering reliance on fossil fuels.

Eligibility

  • Farmers seeking new power supply connections.
  • Priority for those who have not previously received assistance under similar programs.
  • Special benefits may apply for SC/ST categories.

How to Apply

  • Visit the Official Website: https://tumkur.nic.in/en/schemes/
  • Locate the Scheme: Find "Solar Irrigation Pumpsets Scheme".
  • Download Application Form: Obtain the form from the portal.
  • Fill in Details: Complete the form accurately.
  • Submit Documents: Attach land ownership proof, identity proof, and other required certificates.
  • Submit Application: Submit to designated office or online portal.

Documents Required

  • Land Ownership Proof: Verify agricultural land ownership.
  • Identity Proof: Aadhaar card or other government-issued ID.
  • Passport-sized Photographs: Recent photos of applicant.
  • Bank Account Details: For subsidy or reimbursement.
  • Category Certificate: SC/ST if applicable.
