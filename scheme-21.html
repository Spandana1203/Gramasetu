Deendayal Upadhyaya Gram Jyothi Yojana (DDUGJY)

Objective: Provide continuous and reliable electricity supply to rural India, replacing the earlier Rajiv Gandhi Grameen Vidyutikaran Yojana, focusing on feeder separation, infrastructure strengthening, and metering.

Benefits

  • Universal electrification: Every village and household will be electrified.
  • Economic growth: Stimulates growth of small and household enterprises, creating employment.
  • Agricultural productivity: Reliable power supply increases agriculture yield.
  • Improved services: Enhances access to telephone, radio, internet, TV, mobile, education, health, and banking services.
  • Community development: Electricity access to panchayats, schools, police stations, hospitals, improving social security.
  • Comprehensive rural development: Increases opportunities for economic and social development in rural areas.

Eligibility

  • All DISCOMs, including State Power Departments and private sector DISCOMs, are eligible.
  • Private sector DISCOMs in rural areas implement projects via State Government agency.
  • State Government or State-owned companies own assets created under the scheme.
  • Rural Electric (RE) Cooperative Societies can undertake projects but must submit annual audited utilization statements.

How to Apply / Process

  • Managed at state level; State Governments and DISCOMs prepare Need Assessment Document (NAD) and Detailed Project Reports (DPRs).
  • Proposals submitted online to Ministry of Power, which approves and releases funds.
  • Projects implemented on a turnkey basis with a tripartite agreement between Ministry of Power (REC), DISCOM, and State Government.

Documents Required (for Agencies/Implementers)

  • Need Assessment Document (NAD)
  • Detailed Project Report (DPR) with technical specifications, budget, timeline
  • Tripartite Agreement
  • Annual Audited Statements (for RE Cooperative Societies)
  • State Government approvals/clearances
  • Supporting maps and technical drawings for substations, feeders, and distribution lines
