Micro Credit Prerana Scheme

Description: Provides microcredit facilities to women from economically weaker sections, particularly those associated with SHGs. Encourages entrepreneurship and financial independence.

Benefits

  • Loans of ‚Çπ10,000 ‚Äì ‚Çπ50,000 through cooperative banks and SHGs.
  • Subsidized interest rates for prompt repayment.
  • Credit linked to income-generating activities.

Eligibility

  • Women must be part of a recognized SHG or cooperative society.
  • Belong to economically weaker sections.
  • Must have a functional savings account.

How to Apply

  • Approach your nearest cooperative bank or SHG group leader.
  • Fill the microcredit application form with SHG details.
  • Attach required documents (SHG registration, ID proofs, income proof).
  • Submit to the bank or cooperative society for verification.
  • Receive approval and disbursement in SHG account.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • SHG Registration Certificate
  • Bank Account Details
  • Income Proof
  • Residential Proof
  • Passport-size Photograph
