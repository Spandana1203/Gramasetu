Swachh Bharat Gramin Karnataka

Description: Part of the national Swachh Bharat Mission aimed at improving sanitation and hygiene in rural Karnataka. Supports construction of household toilets, solid & liquid waste management, and awareness campaigns for ODF villages.

Eligibility

  • Rural households in Karnataka without individual toilets.
  • Priority: BPL families, SC/ST households, women-headed families.
  • Villages and Gram Panchayats for community sanitation projects.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for individual household toilets.
  • Improved sanitation in schools, Anganwadis, and Panchayat buildings.
  • Village-level solid and liquid waste management systems.
  • Health benefits through reduced waterborne diseases.
  • Behavioral change towards cleanliness and hygiene.

How to Apply

  1. Visit Swachh Bharat Gramin Portal.
  2. Register using Aadhaar and select Karnataka (Rural) as your state.
  3. Fill household or Gram Panchayat details and sanitation requirements.
  4. Upload documents: Aadhaar, residence proof, and BPL card (if applicable).
  5. Submit application for verification by local Panchayat and Rural Development Department.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Ministry of Jal Shakti in coordination with Karnataka Rural Development & Panchayat Raj Department.
  • Community-led awareness campaigns and Swachhagrahis support behavioral change.
  • Incentives provided via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to bank accounts.
  • Regular monitoring and ODF certification at village level.

Importance

Enhances rural health, sanitation, and water management, promoting social development by achieving Open Defecation Free villages.
