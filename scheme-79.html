Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) ‚Äì Crop Insurance Karnataka

Description: PMFBY provides crop insurance coverage against natural calamities, pests, and diseases. It stabilizes farmer income and promotes risk-free agriculture.

Eligibility

  • All farmers growing notified crops in notified areas of Karnataka.
  • Loanee farmers with crop loans from banks are compulsorily covered.
  • Non-loanee farmers can voluntarily enroll by paying the premium.
  • Farmers must have insurable interest on the notified crop.

Benefits

  • Insurance coverage for Kharif, Rabi, and annual commercial/horticultural crops.
  • Protection against crop losses caused by drought, flood, cyclone, pests, and diseases.
  • Uniform premium rates: 2% Kharif, 1.5% Rabi, 5% horticultural/commercial crops.
  • Remaining premium subsidized by Central and State Governments.
  • Claims transferred directly to farmers‚Äô bank accounts via DBT.

How to Apply

  • Visit official portal: https://pmfby.gov.in or Bangalore Rural Agriculture Portal.
  • Register with Aadhaar, land records (RTC/Pahani), and bank account details.
  • Select the notified crop and season (Kharif/Rabi).
  • Pay farmer‚Äôs share of premium online or at nearest bank/CSC.
  • Receive policy certificate and track claim status online.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, Govt. of India, with Karnataka Agriculture Department support.
  • Risk coverage includes pre-sowing, standing crop, and post-harvest losses.
  • Mobile app available for farmer registration and claim tracking.
  • Awareness campaigns conducted via Raitha Samparka Kendras and Gram Panchayats.

Importance

PMFBY provides financial protection from crop loss, stabilizes income, reduces risk, supports cost management, and promotes agricultural production and stability.
