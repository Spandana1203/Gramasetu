Raitha Siri Scheme (Millet Support) ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: A farmer welfare initiative promoting millet cultivation (ragi, jowar, foxtail millet) with financial incentives to enhance food security, sustainable agriculture, and farmer income.

Eligibility

  • Farmers cultivating millets in Karnataka.
  • Small and marginal farmers eligible.
  • Must have cultivable land with proof of ownership (RTC/Pahani).
  • Preference to farmers in dryland or rainfed regions.

Benefits

  • Incentive of ‚Çπ10,000 per hectare for millet cultivation.
  • Promotion of millets as ‚Äúnutri-cereals‚Äù for better health.
  • Improved soil fertility and water conservation.
  • Market support and assistance linking farmers to processing industries.
  • Boost to farmer income and reduced climate risk.

How to Apply

  • Visit nearest Raitha Samparka Kendra (RSK) or Taluk Agriculture Office.
  • Register with Aadhaar, bank account, and land documents.
  • Fill application for Raitha Siri incentives under millet cultivation.
  • Submit Aadhaar, RTC/Pahani, and bank passbook copy.
  • Financial incentive credited directly via DBT to bank account.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Department of Agriculture, Karnataka.
  • Part of Karnataka Millet Mission, aligned with national millet promotion initiatives.
  • Supports nutritional security and climate-smart agriculture.
  • Special training and workshops conducted to promote millet farming and marketing.

Importance

Raitha Siri Scheme promotes sustainable millet cultivation, enhances farmer income, ensures nutritional security, reduces climate risk, and strengthens market linkages.
