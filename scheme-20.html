Bhagyalakshmi Girl Child Welfare Scheme

Objective: Encourages girl child birth in BPL families, improves social status, and provides financial assistance for upbringing and education.

Eligibility

  • Residency: Karnataka resident
  • BPL family with valid BPL card
  • Maximum of 2 girl children per family
  • Girl child born after March 31, 2006
  • Birth registered within 1 year
  • Child should not be engaged in child labour
  • Immunized as per health schedule
  • Education: At least up to 8th standard and not married before 18 years

Benefits

  • Financial Assistance: Deposit in girl's name at birth; maturity amount at 18 years
  • Scholarships: ‚Çπ300‚Äì‚Çπ1,000 per year from 1st to 10th standard
  • Health Insurance: Up to ‚Çπ25,000 per year
  • Accident Benefits: Compensation in case of accidental or natural death

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate of girl child
  • BPL Card of family
  • Parents' Income Certificate
  • Parents' ID & Address Proof (Aadhaar, Ration Card)
  • Bank Account Details of girl child
  • Photographs of girl child and parents
  • Immunization Records of girl child

How to Apply

  • Offline: Obtain form from Anganwadi/Gram Panchayat/DD Women & Child Development office, fill, attach documents, submit
  • Online Status Check: Visit http://blakshmi.kar.nic.in, select "Query Search ‚Üí Multi Search", enter name, DOB, application ID
