Pragati Patha Rural Road Connectivity Program ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: State initiative to enhance rural road infrastructure and provide all-weather connectivity between villages and essential services. Complements PMGSY by covering additional villages and upgrading roads.

Eligibility

  • Rural habitations not covered under PMGSY or requiring road upgradation.
  • Villages with population 100+ in backward, hilly, and drought-prone areas.
  • Projects proposed by Gram Panchayats and approved by DRDAs.

Benefits

  • Construction of all-weather rural roads in unconnected or partially connected villages.
  • Upgradation of existing kutcha/damaged roads into pucca (paved) roads.
  • Reduced travel time and transportation costs.
  • Improved access to education, healthcare, markets, and government services.
  • Boosts local economy via better transport of agricultural produce.

How to Apply

  • No individual application needed.
  • Gram Panchayats identify road needs and propose them in Gram Sabha meetings.
  • Proposals submitted to Taluk/District Panchayats for approval.
  • Sanctioned projects implemented by PWD & RDPR, Karnataka.

Additional Details

  • Funded by Government of Karnataka and rural infrastructure grants.
  • Emphasis on eco-friendly materials and sustainable construction practices.
  • Road maintenance via Panchayat-level funds and state grants.
  • Aligned with Chief Minister‚Äôs Rural Infrastructure Development Plan.
