Project Nemmadi (e-Governance Centers)

Description: Launched in 2006 by the Karnataka government, Project Nemmadi provides citizen services via computerized e-Governance centers across villages. It aims to make government services simple, transparent, and corruption-free.

Benefits

  • Easy access to government services close to home in villages
  • Reduced need to visit district headquarters or taluk offices
  • Faster, transparent, and corruption-free delivery of certificates
  • Over 45 services including income, caste, birth/death certificates, agricultural documents, pension applications, land records

Eligibility

  • Any resident of Karnataka
  • Services available in both urban and rural areas, with focus on rural

How to Apply / Use

  • Visit the nearest Nemmadi Kendra (e-Governance center) in your village/taluk
  • Provide required details/documents for the service
  • Pay the nominal service fee
  • Receive certificate/document within Sakala Services Act timelines

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card (identity proof)
  • Residence proof (ration card, voter ID, electricity bill)
  • Service-specific documents:
    • Birth/death proof for birth/death certificate
    • Income proof for income certificate
    • Caste/community proof for caste certificate
    • Land/property documents for land record requests
