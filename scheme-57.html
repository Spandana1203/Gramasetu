Janasevaka Scheme ‚Äì Home Delivery of Government Services

Description: Citizen-centric flagship program of Karnataka Government bringing selected government services directly to the doorstep. Trained Janasevaka assists with document verification, collection, and submission, reducing delays, corruption, and need to visit offices.

Eligibility

  • Residents of Karnataka in serviceable urban areas where Janasevaka operates.
  • Applicant must request services via official portal, mobile app, or helpline.
  • Required documents vary based on the service.

Benefits

  • Doorstep delivery of selected government services.
  • Hassle-free application and verification from home.
  • Saves time, effort, and travel costs.
  • Provides transparency and accountability.
  • Application status tracking online or via SMS.
  • Special focus on senior citizens, differently-abled persons, and working families.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar or government-issued photo ID.
  • Address proof (ration card, voter ID, utility bill, etc.).
  • Service-specific documents (caste certificate, birth certificate, income proof, etc.).
  • Passport-size photographs.
  • Bank details (for DBT-based services).

How to Apply

  1. Call helpline 080-4455-4455 or use Mobile One App / Janasevaka portal.
  2. Select required service from the list of government services.
  3. Book a slot with date and time for Janasevaka visit.
  4. Keep original and photocopy of necessary documents ready.
  5. Janasevaka visits home, verifies, collects, and submits documents.
  6. Pay nominal service charge if applicable.
  7. Track application status online or via SMS until service delivery.

Importance

  • Brings government services to citizens‚Äô doorstep.
  • Ensures dignity for senior citizens, women, and differently-abled.
  • Reduces corruption by eliminating middlemen.
  • Enhances transparency and trust between citizens and government.
  • Saves time and effort for working-class families.
  • Promotes a digital and accessible Karnataka.
