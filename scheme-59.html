Ambedkar Vasati Yojana ‚Äì SC/ST Housing Support

Description: Karnataka Government initiative under the Social Welfare Department to provide safe and permanent housing for economically weaker SC/ST families. Offers financial assistance and subsidies for new construction or upgradation of kutcha/semi-pucca houses.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must belong to SC or ST community.
  • Permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Belong to Below Poverty Line (BPL) category.
  • Family should not own a pucca house or have received housing benefits from other schemes.
  • Land/house ownership documents in applicant‚Äôs name are mandatory.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for new house construction.
  • Subsidy support for repairing/upgrading kutcha/semi-pucca houses.
  • Direct transfer of sanctioned amounts into bank account in stages.
  • Priority for BPL SC/ST beneficiaries.
  • Technical assistance and monitoring during construction.
  • Promotes better living standards, hygiene, and safety.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card (linked with bank account).
  • Caste certificate and income certificate from competent authority.
  • BPL ration card.
  • Land ownership documents or property records.
  • Bank account details (Aadhaar-seeded passbook copy).
  • Passport-size photographs.
  • Residence proof (ration card/voter ID, etc.).

How to Apply

  1. Visit Seva Sindhu Portal ‚Üí https://sevasindhugs.karnataka.gov.in.
  2. Register as a new user or log in.
  3. Go to ‚ÄúApply for Services‚Äù ‚Üí Select Ambedkar Vasati Yojana.
  4. Fill in personal, income, and land ownership details.
  5. Upload required documents in prescribed format.
  6. Verify Aadhaar OTP and submit the application.
  7. Note down the application number for tracking.
  8. Local officials verify application; funds are released in stages after site inspection.

Importance

  • Provides shelter and security to marginalized SC/ST families.
  • Improves living conditions and ensures dignity.
  • Reduces inequality by bridging the housing gap.
  • Allows families to focus on education, health, and livelihoods.
  • Strengthens the vision of ‚ÄúHousing for All‚Äù in Karnataka.
