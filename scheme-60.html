Saptapadi Vivaha Yojana ‚Äì Mass Marriage Scheme

Description: Karnataka Government initiative promoting simple mass marriages to reduce financial burden on families. Provides financial assistance and marriage kits to couples at government-organized mass marriage events.

Eligibility

  • Bride and groom must be permanent residents of Karnataka.
  • Legal age: Bride 18+ years, Groom 21+ years.
  • Couples must register for the mass marriage event under this scheme.
  • Family income should be within limits prescribed by Social Welfare/Revenue Department (usually BPL/APL families).
  • First marriage only (scheme not applicable for remarriages).

Benefits

  • One-time financial assistance for the couple.
  • Marriage kit including household essentials.
  • Mass marriage ceremony arranged at notified temples/community halls.
  • Financial assistance directly transferred to bride‚Äôs bank account.
  • Reduces financial burden, promotes equality and social harmony.
  • Discourages dowry practices.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar cards of bride and groom.
  • Age proof (SSLC marks card, birth certificate, or other valid ID).
  • Income certificate from competent authority.
  • Residence proof (ration card, voter ID, etc.).
  • Passport-size photographs of bride and groom.
  • Joint marriage application/affidavit.
  • Bank account details of the bride (Aadhaar-linked).

How to Apply

  1. Visit Seva Sindhu Portal ‚Üí https://sevasindhugs.karnataka.gov.in.
  2. Register as a new user or log in.
  3. Go to ‚ÄúApply for Services‚Äù ‚Üí Select Saptapadi Vivaha Yojana.
  4. Fill details of bride and groom and attach income/residence documents.
  5. Upload all required documents in prescribed format.
  6. Complete Aadhaar OTP verification and submit application.
  7. Couples must attend the notified mass marriage event at designated location.
  8. After completion, financial assistance and marriage kit are provided.

Importance

  • Reduces financial stress on poor families.
  • Promotes simple weddings and discourages dowry.
  • Encourages community bonding and equality.
  • Ensures dignity and social support for couples.
  • Contributes to reducing debts incurred for wedding expenses.
