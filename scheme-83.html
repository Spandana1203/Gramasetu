Swavalambi Sarathi Scheme (Minority Driver Subsidy) ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: Welfare program supporting unemployed minority youth to purchase vehicles and promote self-employment in transport, helping beneficiaries become financially independent.

Eligibility

  • Belong to a minority community (Muslims, Christians, Jains, Buddhists, Sikhs, Parsis).
  • Age: 18‚Äì45 years.
  • Annual family income ‚â§ ‚Çπ4.5 lakh.
  • Valid driving license for vehicle category applied.
  • Preference: unemployed youth and first-time vehicle owners.

Benefits

  • Subsidy up to 50% of vehicle cost (max ‚Çπ3 lakh).
  • Encourages self-employment via taxis, autorickshaws, goods carriers, or other commercial vehicles.
  • Reduces financial burden on minority youth in transport sector.
  • Improves livelihood and income generation in rural and urban areas.
  • Promotes entrepreneurship among minority communities.

How to Apply

  • Visit: Official Portal.
  • Register with Aadhaar, income certificate, caste/minority certificate, driving license details.
  • Upload Aadhaar, residence proof, income certificate, caste/minority certificate, driving license, bank account details.
  • Submit online application; verification by Minorities Welfare Department, Karnataka.
  • Subsidy released directly to vehicle dealer/bank after loan sanction.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Karnataka Minorities Development Corporation (KMDC).
  • Apply individually or with banks for vehicle loans.
  • Beneficiaries encouraged to register on Ola, Uber, and logistics platforms.
  • Scheme aims to create sustainable self-employment opportunities in transport and service sectors.
