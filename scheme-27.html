Paduke Kuteera Housing Scheme

Description: Provides affordable housing support to economically weaker families who lack proper residential facilities. Implemented under Karnataka‚Äôs Ashraya Housing initiatives.

Benefits

  • Subsidized housing units or grants for building houses.
  • Financial support up to ‚Çπ2.5 lakh per family depending on construction type.

Eligibility

  • Permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Annual family income below prescribed limit (‚Çπ32,000‚Äì‚Çπ1.2 lakh).
  • Should not have benefited from other housing schemes.

How to Apply

  • Visit ashraya.karnataka.gov.in.
  • Register and log in to the Ashraya housing portal.
  • Fill application with family income and housing details.
  • Upload documents: Aadhaar, income certificate, BPL card, etc.
  • Submit application and track status online.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income Certificate
  • BPL Ration Card
  • Residential Proof (Karnataka domicile)
  • No-house Ownership Certificate
  • Passport-size Photograph

Additional Details

  • Priority to SC/ST, widows, disabled, and BPL families.
  • Physical verification by Panchayat is mandatory.
