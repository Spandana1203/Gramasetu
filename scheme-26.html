Charmakara Housing Scheme

Description: Provides financial assistance to economically weaker families of the Charmakara community (SC) for house construction or improvement, ensuring safe and dignified living conditions.

Benefits

  • Subsidy: ‚Çπ1.2 lakh to ‚Çπ2 lakh depending on category and cost index.
  • Priority to BPL card holders and families without permanent housing.
  • Community empowerment through improved living standards.

Eligibility

  • Belong to the Charmakara caste (SC category).
  • Permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Annual family income below government-notified limit (usually ‚Çπ2.5 lakh).
  • Family should not already own a pucca house.

How to Apply

  • Visit swd.karnataka.gov.in
  • Go to Schemes section and select "Charmakara Housing Scheme".
  • Register using Aadhaar and mobile number.
  • Fill in income, caste, and land ownership details.
  • Upload required documents.
  • Submit application and note the acknowledgement number.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate (Charmakara)
  • Income Certificate
  • BPL Ration Card
  • Land Ownership Proof
  • Recent Passport-size Photograph

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Karnataka Social Welfare Department.
  • Priority given to BPL and families without permanent housing.
