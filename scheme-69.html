Raitha Shakti Scheme (Diesel Subsidy for Farmers)

Description: Karnataka Government farmer welfare initiative providing diesel subsidy to reduce cultivation and irrigation costs. Encourages mechanized farming, modern agricultural practices, and higher profitability.

Eligibility

  • Farmer and permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Ownership of cultivable agricultural land (verified via RTC/land records).
  • Valid Aadhaar-linked bank account.
  • Priority to small and marginal farmers.
  • Should not be a defaulter in any government subsidy scheme.

Benefits

  • ‚Çπ250 per acre diesel subsidy (subject to government updates).
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to Aadhaar-linked bank account.
  • Reduces fuel expenses for tractors, pump sets, and farm machinery.
  • Encourages mechanized farming for efficiency and productivity.
  • Helps small and marginal farmers reduce input costs.
  • Transparent and hassle-free subsidy distribution via Seva Sindhu.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card (linked with bank account).
  • RTC / land ownership documents.
  • Residence proof (ration card, voter ID, etc.).
  • Bank passbook copy (Aadhaar-seeded).
  • Passport-size photographs.
  • Application form/self-declaration of diesel use for farming.

How to Apply

  1. Visit nearest Raitha Samparka Kendra (RSK) or Agriculture Department office.
  2. Collect and fill out Raitha Shakti Scheme application form.
  3. Attach land records, Aadhaar, and bank details.
  4. Submit form to Agriculture Officer at RSK.
  5. Verification based on land records and farmer category.
  6. Approved beneficiaries receive subsidy through DBT.
  7. Apply and track online via Seva Sindhu Portal.

Importance

  • Reduces diesel expenses for cultivation and irrigation.
  • Encourages mechanization in agriculture.
  • Improves productivity and operational efficiency.
  • Benefits small and marginal farmers by lowering input costs.
  • Supports Karnataka‚Äôs mission of doubling farmers‚Äô income.
