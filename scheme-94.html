Kalyana Patha: Rural Infrastructure Upgrade ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: Scheme by RDPR to enhance rural infrastructure and improve living standards in villages. Covers roads, drinking water, sanitation, street lighting, and community assets.

Eligibility

  • All Gram Panchayats in Karnataka are eligible.
  • Priority to backward regions, drought-prone districts, and villages lacking basic amenities.
  • Projects must be proposed by Gram Panchayats and approved by Taluk/District Panchayat.

Benefits

  • Construction and upgradation of roads, drainage, and water supply lines.
  • Development of Anganwadis, schools, and primary health centers.
  • Provision of street lighting and sanitation facilities.
  • Boosts agricultural productivity and local economy.
  • Reduces rural-to-urban migration by improving living conditions.

How to Apply

  • Gram Panchayats identify infrastructure needs via Gram Sabha meetings.
  • Prepare proposals and submit to Taluk/District Panchayat offices.
  • Approved projects sanctioned by RDPR; funds released accordingly.
  • Implementation and monitoring carried out at Panchayat and district levels.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by RDPR, Government of Karnataka.
  • Works in convergence with PMGSY, Jal Jeevan Mission, Swachh Bharat Mission.
  • Funds allocated from state budget and monitored via online portals.
  • Encourages community participation in planning and maintenance of rural assets.
