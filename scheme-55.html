Spoorthi Scheme ‚Äì Financial Support for Rural Entrepreneurs

Description: Karnataka Government initiative supporting rural entrepreneurship by providing financial assistance, subsidies, and training for individuals, women, and SHGs to start or expand micro and small enterprises.

Eligibility

  • Permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Belongs to eligible categories: SC/ST, OBC, minorities, women, or SHG members.
  • Must propose a viable business plan/project.
  • Age between 18‚Äì55 years (as notified).
  • Family income within prescribed limit.
  • Should not be a defaulter in any financial institution.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance via loans with subsidy or interest concessions.
  • Support for setting up new micro-enterprises or expanding existing ones.
  • Training and capacity-building programs for entrepreneurs.
  • Encouragement for women, SC/ST, and marginalized groups.
  • Market linkages and guidance for promotion and sales.
  • Generates local employment and promotes rural development.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card and residence proof.
  • Caste certificate and income certificate.
  • Passport-size photographs.
  • Business plan/proposal with cost estimates.
  • Bank account details (Aadhaar-linked).
  • Educational qualification certificate (if required).
  • SHG/group registration papers (for group-based enterprises).

How to Apply

  1. Visit Seva Sindhu Portal: https://sevasindhugs.karnataka.gov.in
  2. Register as a new user or log in.
  3. Select ‚ÄúApply for Services‚Äù ‚Üí Spoorthi Scheme.
  4. Fill in applicant details, business idea, and project cost.
  5. Upload all required documents in prescribed format.
  6. Complete Aadhaar OTP verification and submit.
  7. Save application number for tracking.
  8. District Social Welfare officials will review and sanction the proposal.

Importance

  • Promotes self-reliance and reduces unemployment.
  • Boosts rural industries, small businesses, and SHGs.
  • Provides equal opportunities for marginalized communities.
  • Strengthens rural economy by generating local jobs.
  • Encourages innovation and entrepreneurship among youth and women.
