Gruha Jyothi Scheme (Electricity Subsidy)

Description: Launched on 1 August 2023 by the Government of Karnataka, it offers eligible households up to 200 units of free electricity per month, subsidized fully by the state.

Benefits

  • Households within their calculated free-usage limit (up to 200 units) receive zero electricity bill.
  • Encourages energy-efficient consumption, potentially saving around ‚Çπ1,000 per month.
  • Low-consumption households (under ~90 units) get an extra 10 units free.
  • Includes a delink facility for households that relocate.

Eligibility

  • Must be a resident of Karnataka with a domestic electricity connection.
  • Consumption should be below 200 units per month, based on past data.
  • Tenants can apply even if the meter is in the landlord‚Äôs name.
  • Aadhaar must be linked to the electricity account.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card
  • Electricity bill or Connection ID
  • Rental agreement (for tenants)

How to Apply (Online ‚Äì Seva Sindhu Portal)

  • Visit Seva Sindhu Portal and click on ‚ÄúGruha Jyothi Scheme‚Äù.
  • Fill details: ESCOM, Account ID, Owner/Tenant, Aadhaar, Mobile, etc.
  • Verify via OTP, agree to declaration, and submit.
  • You'll receive an acknowledgement via SMS or email.
  • Check your application status using your Account ID or application number on the portal.
