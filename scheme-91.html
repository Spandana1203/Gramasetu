Arivu Kendra Digital Libraries in Villages

Description: Initiative to set up modern digital libraries with internet access, e-learning resources, and digital devices in rural areas. Students, farmers, and rural youth can access educational content, government services, and skill development resources.

Eligibility

  • Residents of rural Karnataka (students, farmers, unemployed youth, SHG members).
  • Priority for students preparing for board exams, higher education, and competitive exams.
  • Open to all community members without restrictions.

Benefits

  • Free access to computers, internet, and digital learning platforms.
  • Availability of e-books, online courses, and competitive exam material.
  • Support for school and college students with study resources and digital literacy programs.
  • Farmers can access real-time information on crops, weather, and government schemes.
  • Promotes lifelong learning, skill enhancement, and digital inclusion in villages.

How to Apply

  • No formal application needed for basic access. Visit the local Arivu Kendra.
  • Membership registration may be required for borrowing digital devices or accessing premium resources.
  • Panchayats maintain a register of users for monitoring and support.
  • For advanced training or courses, register online through the Karnataka Education Department portal.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by RDPR and Department of Education, Karnataka.
  • Centers established in Panchayat offices, schools, and community halls.
  • Linked with Karnataka Digital Learning Portals and Grama One centers.
  • Encourages rural youth to adopt technology for education, employment, and entrepreneurship.
