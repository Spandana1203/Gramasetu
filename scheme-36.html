Chief Minister Grama Vikasa Scheme

Description: The Chief Minister Grama Vikasa Scheme is focused on improving infrastructure and basic amenities in rural areas of Karnataka. It emphasizes balanced rural development by addressing gaps in facilities such as roads, drinking water, sanitation, schools, and health centers.

Benefits

  • Development of rural roads, drainage, and sanitation.
  • Construction of anganwadis, schools, and primary health centers.
  • Electrification and water supply projects.
  • Social and community halls to strengthen rural participation.

Eligibility

  • Any rural Gram Panchayat can propose works under this scheme.
  • Residents of the Panchayat who attend Gram Sabha meetings can raise demands.
  • Priority given to villages lacking basic amenities or falling under backward categories.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Panchayat application form
  • Project proposal (for development work)
  • Voter ID / Residence proof

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://rdpr.karnataka.gov.in.
  2. Approach your Gram Panchayat office for scheme registration.
  3. Submit a proposal or request for development works.
  4. Panchayat verifies and includes it in the CMGV plan.
