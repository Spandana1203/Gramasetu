Maithreyi Yojana ‚Äì Widow Marriage Financial Support

Description: Karnataka Government social welfare scheme providing financial assistance to widows who remarry, ensuring dignity, economic security, and social empowerment.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must be a widow (verified by legal documents).
  • Permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Marriage legally registered under Karnataka Marriage Registration Act.
  • Family income within limit specified by Social Welfare Department.
  • Caste/community subcategories (SC/ST/General) may apply as per government notification.

Benefits

  • One-time financial assistance to newly married couple.
  • Direct transfer to beneficiary bank account.
  • Encourages social acceptance of widow remarriage.
  • Supports setting up a new household.
  • Promotes inclusivity, dignity, and rehabilitation for widows.

Documents Required

  • Widowhood certificate or legal proof (husband‚Äôs death certificate)
  • Marriage registration certificate of the new marriage
  • Caste & income certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhaar cards of bride and groom (linked with bank accounts)
  • Bank passbook copy (Aadhaar-seeded)
  • Passport-size photographs of couple
  • Residence proof (ration card, voter ID, etc.)

How to Apply

  1. Visit Seva Sindhu Portal: https://sevasindhugs.karnataka.gov.in
  2. Register as a new user or log in.
  3. Select ‚ÄúApply for Services‚Äù ‚Üí Maithreyi Yojana.
  4. Fill applicant and spouse details along with marriage details.
  5. Upload required documents in prescribed format.
  6. Complete Aadhaar OTP verification and submit.
  7. Note application number and track status online.

Importance

  • Breaks societal stigma around widow remarriage.
  • Provides financial support for rebuilding lives.
  • Promotes equality and justice for women.
  • Ensures emotional and economic stability for widows starting anew.
  • Reaffirms the state‚Äôs commitment to dignity, security, and happiness for women.
