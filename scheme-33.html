Atal Pension Yojana (APY)

Description: APY is a national pension scheme targeting unorganized sector workers. It provides a guaranteed monthly pension after the age of 60, based on contributions made during the working years.

Benefits

  • Guaranteed monthly pension between ‚Çπ1,000 and ‚Çπ5,000.
  • Pension amount based on contributions (‚Çπ42‚Äì‚Çπ210 per month).
  • Spouse receives pension benefits if the subscriber dies.

Eligibility

  • Age between 18‚Äì40 years.
  • Must have a savings bank account.
  • Not already covered under any statutory pension scheme.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Bank Account Passbook
  • Mobile Number linked to Aadhaar
  • Passport-size Photograph

How to Apply

  1. Visit any bank branch or post office where you hold an account.
  2. Fill out the APY registration form with age and contribution details.
  3. Link Aadhaar and bank account for auto-debit.
  4. Choose the pension amount (‚Çπ1,000 to ‚Çπ5,000 per month).
  5. Submit and get an acknowledgement slip.
