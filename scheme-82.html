Gramin Bhandaran Yojana (Rural Storage Subsidy) ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: Scheme promoting construction and modernization of rural godowns to provide scientific storage facilities, reduce post-harvest losses, and help farmers get better market prices.

Eligibility

  • Individual farmers, farmer groups, SHGs, cooperatives, and agricultural entrepreneurs.
  • Panchayats, NGOs, and private companies engaged in agriculture.
  • Godown capacity must be 100‚Äì30,000 MT.

Benefits

  • Subsidy for construction or renovation of rural godowns.
  • Safe storage of produce and avoidance of distress sales.
  • Scientific storage improves shelf life and quality of grains and seeds.
  • Access to loans against stored produce.
  • Reduces post-harvest losses and enhances food security.

How to Apply

  • Visit nearest Agriculture Department or NABARD office.
  • Submit proposal with land documents, building plan, and cost estimates.
  • Register with Aadhaar, PAN, and bank details.
  • Apply through NABARD or scheduled banks for subsidy-cum-loan.
  • After construction, subsidy (25%‚Äì33%) credited to beneficiary‚Äôs loan account.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by NABARD with support from Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare.
  • Subsidy levels: 33% for SC/ST, women, SHGs; 25% for others.
  • Godowns can store food grains, seeds, fertilizers, and inputs.
  • Promotes pledge financing and negotiable warehouse receipt system.
