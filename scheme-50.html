Prabhuddha Overseas Scholarship

Description: A flagship scheme by Karnataka Government offering comprehensive financial support to SC/ST students pursuing postgraduate or doctoral programs abroad. It empowers students with global exposure, research opportunities, and skill development.

Eligibility

  • Permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Belong to SC/ST community.
  • Valid admission letter from recognized foreign university.
  • Passed Graduate or Postgraduate degree with good academic record.
  • Family income not exceeding ‚Çπ8 lakhs (as per notification).
  • Preference to students admitted to top-ranking universities.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for tuition, living costs, and one-time airfare.
  • Support for visa, insurance, and contingencies.
  • Applicable for postgraduate, doctoral, and professional courses.
  • Enhances employability and global exposure.
  • Promotes leadership, research, and academic excellence.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card (linked with bank account)
  • Caste and Income Certificate issued by competent authority
  • Admission/Offer letter from foreign university
  • Academic transcripts (SSLC, PUC, Degree, PG)
  • Passport & valid Visa documents
  • Bank account details (Aadhaar-linked)
  • Statement of Purpose / Research Proposal (if applicable)
  • Passport-size photos

How to Apply

  1. Visit Seva Sindhu Portal.
  2. Register as a new user or log in.
  3. Navigate to Apply for Services ‚Üí Select ‚ÄúPrabhuddha Overseas Scholarship‚Äù.
  4. Fill personal, educational, course, and bank details.
  5. Upload required documents in specified format.
  6. Verify Aadhaar OTP and submit application.
  7. Take note of the application number and track status on Seva Sindhu.

Importance

  • Promotes social justice and empowerment of marginalized students.
  • Fosters educational equity and global competitiveness.
  • Enhances Karnataka's human capital in science, technology, research, and innovation.
