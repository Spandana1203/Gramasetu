Labour Card Scholarship

Description: The Karnataka Building and Other Construction Workers Welfare Board provides scholarships to children of registered construction workers to support their education.

Eligibility

  • Parent must be a registered construction worker under KBOCWWB.
  • Student must be enrolled in school, PUC, or higher education.
  • Family income should be within welfare board guidelines.

Benefits

  • Scholarship ranges from ‚Çπ2,000 for school students to ‚Çπ11,000 for professional courses.
  • Encourages children of labourers to continue education.
  • Covers tuition and exam fees.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Labour Card (parent registered under KBOCWWB)
  • Student‚Äôs marks card
  • School/college bonafide certificate
  • Bank account details

How to Apply

Visit KBOCWWB Education Portal. Log in with Labour ID, fill student details, upload marks card, and submit application. Track status online.
