Amrith School Infrastructure Project ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: State-level initiative to improve educational facilities in government schools across rural areas, providing safe classrooms, proper sanitation, drinking water, digital tools, and playgrounds.

Eligibility

  • Government primary and high schools in Karnataka, especially rural and underdeveloped areas.
  • Priority for schools lacking basic infrastructure or in backward districts.
  • Proposals submitted by Gram Panchayats, SDMCs, or Education Department.

Benefits

  • Construction and renovation of classrooms, libraries, laboratories.
  • Provision of toilets, drinking water, and electricity.
  • Installation of digital learning tools like smart boards and computer labs.
  • Development of playgrounds and sports facilities.
  • Enhanced quality of education and reduced dropout rates.

How to Apply

  • Submit infrastructure requirements via Block Education Officer (BEO) or District Education Officer (DEO).
  • Proposals forwarded to Department of School Education and Literacy, Karnataka for approval.
  • Funds allocated after verification and priority assessment.
  • Community participation encouraged through SDMCs for monitoring and maintenance.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Department of School Education and Literacy, Karnataka Government.
  • Aligned with central schemes like Samagra Shiksha Abhiyan.
  • Funding via state budget, CSR initiatives, and Panchayat contributions.
  • Regular monitoring ensures timely completion and quality standards.
