Karnataka ePass Scholarship

Description: The Karnataka ePass (Electronic Payment and Application System of Scholarships) provides scholarships to students belonging to SC, ST, and OBC categories. It supports post-matric education with direct benefit transfer to student bank accounts.

Benefits

  • Scholarship amount ranges from ‚Çπ3,000 to ‚Çπ19,000 depending on course.
  • Fee reimbursement for tuition and hostel charges.
  • Direct Benefit Transfer to student‚Äôs bank account.

Eligibility

  • Resident of Karnataka.
  • Belong to SC/ST/OBC category.
  • Family income: up to ‚Çπ2.5 lakh for SC/ST, ‚Çπ1 lakh for OBC.
  • Must have secured at least 50% marks in the last exam.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate (SC/ST/OBC)
  • Income Certificate
  • Marks card of last exam
  • Bank account details

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://karepass.cgg.gov.in.
  2. Register and log in as a student.
  3. Fill the scholarship application and upload required documents.
  4. Submit and track application online.
