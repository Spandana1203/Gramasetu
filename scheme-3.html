Shakti Free Bus Scheme (for Women)

Description: Launched on June 11, 2023, by the Siddaramaiah government. It's the first of Karnataka's five flagship ‚ÄúGuarantee Schemes.‚Äù Women who are Karnataka domiciles can travel free of charge on non-luxury state-run buses. For the first three months, they may present a government-issued photo ID and address proof to receive a zero-fare ticket; later, they‚Äôll need a Shakti smartcard issued via the Seva Sindhu portal.

Benefits

  • Zero-fare rides on eligible state-run ordinary, urban, express, and regular buses‚Äîno restriction on travel distance within Karnataka.
  • By mid-July 2025, the scheme surpassed 500 crore tickets issued, with expenditure exceeding ‚Çπ12,600 crore.
  • Daily patronage increased from ~85 lakh to 1 crore+ riders per day, improving access to education, work, and other services for women.
  • In Mysuru district alone, 31 crore+ women benefited between June 2023 and June 2025, significantly boosting RTC revenue.

Eligibility

  • Permanent residents of Karnataka who are women, including female students, girls aged 6‚Äì12, and members of the transgender community.
  • Valid only for non-premium intra-state bus services.
  • Luxury, AC, sleeper, premium (e.g., Airavat, Vajra), and interstate buses are excluded‚Äîeven if they travel within the state.

How to Apply (Smart Card)

  • Visit Seva Sindhu Portal.
  • Register or log in using your details.
  • Click ‚ÄúApply for Shakti Smart Card,‚Äù fill in the form, and upload a photo ID with address (e.g., Aadhaar, Voter ID, DL).
  • Your application will be verified, and once approved, the Shakti Smart Card is issued.
  • Until you receive the card, you can travel for free by showing a supported photo ID.
  • Track your application status on the Seva Sindhu portal.
  • For assistance, call the helpline at 1902.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card ‚Äì for identity & age proof
  • Voter ID / Ration Card ‚Äì for domicile & address proof (Karnataka residency)
