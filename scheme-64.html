Shrama Samarthya Yojane ‚Äì Skill Support for Workers

Description: Karnataka Government initiative aimed at improving skills of workers in organized and unorganized sectors. Provides training, certification, and upskilling to enhance employability, productivity, and access to livelihood opportunities.

Eligibility

  • Permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Workers in organized or unorganized sectors.
  • Age 18‚Äì45 years (may vary by program).
  • Priority to unorganized workers, women, SC/ST, minorities, and economically weaker sections.
  • Willingness to undergo training and skill certification.

Benefits

  • Free or subsidized skill development and vocational training.
  • Skill certification under recognized state/national frameworks.
  • Focus on unorganized workers, women, and youth.
  • Employment assistance via job fairs, counseling, and placements.
  • Financial support/stipends during training for eligible groups.
  • Encouragement for entrepreneurship and self-employment.
  • Builds a stronger, skilled workforce for Karnataka‚Äôs economy.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card (linked with bank account).
  • Residence proof (ration card, voter ID, etc.).
  • Caste and income certificate (for category-based benefits).
  • Passport-size photographs.
  • Bank account details (Aadhaar-seeded).
  • Educational qualification certificates (if applicable).
  • Worker registration card (if already registered under welfare boards).

How to Apply

  1. Visit Seva Sindhu Portal ‚Üí https://sevasindhugs.karnataka.gov.in
  2. Register as new user or log in.
  3. Select ‚ÄúApply for Services‚Äù ‚Üí Shrama Samarthya Yojane.
  4. Fill in background, work, and training preferences.
  5. Upload required documents.
  6. Submit and complete Aadhaar OTP verification.
  7. Applicants shortlisted for training through district-level selection.
  8. Successful candidates notified of schedules, venues, and stipends.

Importance

  • Enhances employability and productivity of Karnataka‚Äôs workforce.
  • Provides livelihood opportunities for marginalized communities.
  • Reduces unemployment and underemployment in rural and urban areas.
  • Prepares youth for industry-relevant jobs in agriculture, manufacturing, and services.
  • Builds a culture of lifelong learning and continuous skill improvement.
