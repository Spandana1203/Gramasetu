Vrutti Protsaha Loan Scheme (Minority Self-Employment) ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: KMDC initiative to promote self-employment among minority communities by providing easy loans with subsidies for starting small businesses or service ventures.

Eligibility

  • Belong to a notified minority community (Muslim, Christian, Jain, Buddhist, Sikh, Parsi).
  • Age: 18‚Äì55 years.
  • Annual family income: Urban ‚â§ ‚Çπ3.5 lakh, Rural ‚â§ ‚Çπ2.5 lakh.
  • No defaults in previous KMDC schemes or bank loans.

Benefits

  • Loan assistance up to ‚Çπ2 lakh for self-employment ventures.
  • Subsidy of 33% of loan amount, maximum ‚Çπ50,000.
  • Supports activities like petty shops, tailoring, handicrafts, dairy, poultry, and micro enterprises.
  • Encourages entrepreneurship among minority youth, women, and small business owners.
  • Low-interest loans with flexible repayment options.

How to Apply

  • Visit: Official KMDC Portal.
  • Register with Aadhaar, income certificate, caste/minority certificate, and bank account details.
  • Select the Vrutti Protsaha Loan Scheme under KMDC schemes.
  • Upload Aadhaar, residence proof, income certificate, caste certificate, and project/business proposal.
  • Submit application; verification by District Minority Welfare Office.
  • Subsidy released directly after loan sanction through partnering banks.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Karnataka Minorities Development Corporation (KMDC).
  • Loans routed through nationalized and cooperative banks.
  • Priority given to women entrepreneurs, unemployed youth, and rural artisans.
  • Supports both traditional occupations and modern service businesses.
