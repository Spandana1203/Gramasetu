Land Purchase Scheme

Description: This scheme assists SC/ST women in purchasing agricultural land by providing financial subsidies. It promotes land ownership and livelihood security among marginalized communities.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance up to ‚Çπ15 lakh (subsidy + loan) for land purchase.
  • 50% subsidy provided by the government, remaining through bank loan.
  • Promotes self-reliance and livelihood security.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must be a woman from SC/ST community.
  • Should not already own agricultural land.
  • Family income must be below ‚Çπ2 lakh per annum.

How to Apply

  1. Visit the Social Welfare Department site: https://sw.kar.nic.in
  2. Register under the Land Purchase Scheme for SC/ST women.
  3. Upload necessary documents: Aadhaar, caste certificate, income proof.
  4. Submit the proposal with land details and ownership documents.
  5. Wait for verification and subsidy approval before purchase.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Caste Certificate (SC/ST)
  • Income Certificate
  • Land Details/Proposal papers
  • Residential Proof
  • Passport-size Photograph
