Pratibha Puraskar Scholarship

Description: The Pratibha Puraskar Scholarship encourages meritorious students from backward classes (SC/ST/OBC) to continue higher education. It provides financial support to students who achieve high marks in their exams.

Benefits

  • Scholarship ranging from ‚Çπ10,000 to ‚Çπ20,000 depending on level of education.
  • One-time financial assistance for educational expenses.
  • Helps in pursuing PUC, graduation, and post-graduation courses.

Eligibility

  • Must belong to SC/ST/OBC category.
  • Should have passed SSLC/PUC/degree with at least 60% marks (relaxation for SC/ST).
  • Family income should not exceed ‚Çπ2.5 lakh for SC/ST and ‚Çπ1‚Äì1.5 lakh for OBCs (as per norms).

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • SSLC/PUC Marks Card
  • Income Certificate
  • Caste Certificate
  • Bank account details

How to Apply

  1. Visit https://ssp.postmatric.karnataka.gov.in.
  2. Create an account and log in.
  3. Fill details and upload marks card, caste and income certificates.
  4. Submit and track application status online.
