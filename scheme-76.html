Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY) ‚Äì Karnataka

Description: PMKSY Karnataka aims to provide efficient irrigation and water management for farmers‚Äô fields, promoting ‚ÄúHar Khet Ko Pani‚Äù and adoption of drip, sprinkler, and watershed technologies.

Eligibility

  • Farmers (individuals, groups, or SHGs) in Karnataka.
  • Priority to small and marginal farmers.
  • Beneficiaries must have cultivable land with a reliable water source.
  • Special preference for SC/ST and women farmers.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance for drip and sprinkler irrigation systems.
  • Improved water use efficiency and reduced wastage.
  • Enhanced agricultural productivity through assured irrigation.
  • Watershed management for soil and water conservation.
  • Training and capacity building in modern irrigation technologies.

How to Apply

  • Visit the official PMKSY portal: https://pmksy.gov.in.
  • Register with Aadhaar, land details, and bank account information.
  • Apply for drip/sprinkler irrigation or watershed project assistance.
  • Upload documents: Aadhaar, RTC, bank passbook, farmer ID.
  • Application verified by Department of Agriculture, Karnataka.

Additional Details

  • Implemented by Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare with Karnataka Agriculture Department support.
  • Convergence of water resources, irrigation, and agriculture programs.
  • Subsidy directly credited via DBT to farmers‚Äô accounts.
  • Awareness and training programs conducted at Raitha Samparka Kendras (RSKs).

Importance

PMKSY Karnataka ensures water security, higher crop productivity, and economic benefits, while teaching modern irrigation and water conservation techniques for rural prosperity.
