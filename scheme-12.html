Pashu Bhagya (Livestock Subsidy)

Objective: To promote livestock farming among small and marginal farmers, enhancing livelihoods and supporting dairy and meat production. Implemented by the Department of Animal Husbandry and Fisheries, Government of Karnataka.

Benefits

  • SC/ST farmers: Up to 50% subsidy on loans up to ‚Çπ1.2 lakh
  • Other small and marginal farmers: Up to 25% subsidy on loans up to ‚Çπ1.2 lakh
  • Dairy cattle: ‚Çπ25,000 per animal
  • Goats and sheep: ‚Çπ1,000 per animal (minimum 10 animals)
  • Poultry: ‚Çπ1,000 subsidy for 100 birds
  • Interest-free short-term loans for cattle feed and maintenance
  • Insurance premium subsidies for up to 5 cattle under Kurigahi Suraksha Scheme
  • Ex-gratia of ‚Çπ5,000 for livestock loss under the same scheme

Eligibility

  • Farmers engaged in livestock rearing
  • Small and marginal farmers
  • Registered with local agriculture or animal husbandry department

How to Apply

Online:

  • Register on the state livestock portal or FRUITS registration system: kawb.karnataka.gov.in
  • Complete the Pashu Bhagya application form
  • Upload necessary documents and submit

Offline:

  • Visit nearest Animal Husbandry Department or Gram Panchayat office
  • Obtain and fill out the application form
  • Submit the form with required documents for verification

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card or government-issued ID
  • Proof of residence (ration card, electricity bill, etc.)
  • Land ownership documents
  • Bank account details for subsidy disbursement
  • Registration certificate with local agriculture/animal husbandry department
  • Recent passport-sized photographs
