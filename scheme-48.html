Raita Vidya Nidhi Scholarship

Description: This scheme provides financial assistance to the children of farmers to pursue higher education. It ensures that financial barriers do not stop students from continuing their studies.

Eligibility

  • Applicant must be a child of a farmer (proof required).
  • Must have passed SSLC/PUC and enrolled in higher studies.
  • Resident of Karnataka.
  • Priority to BPL families.

Benefits

  • Scholarship amount between ‚Çπ2,500 ‚Äì ‚Çπ11,000 depending on the course.
  • Covers undergraduate, professional, and technical courses.
  • Direct Benefit Transfer into student‚Äôs bank account.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Farmer ID (from Agriculture Department)
  • Marks card of last exam
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank details

How to Apply

Visit Raita Mitra Portal. Register, upload Aadhaar, marks card, Farmer ID, and submit application. Track status online.
