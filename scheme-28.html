Kayaka Spoorthi Yojane

Description: Financial assistance and skill development program for members of self-help groups (SHGs), particularly targeting women. Provides loans, training, and business support for self-employment.

Benefits

  • Loans up to ‚Çπ2 lakh at subsidized interest rates.
  • Subsidy of up to 50% for skill-based enterprises.
  • Free training programs in tailoring, handicrafts, dairy, poultry, etc.

Eligibility

  • Women from rural or semi-urban areas.
  • Preferably SC/ST or OBC category.
  • Age: 18‚Äì45 years.

How to Apply

  • Visit kayaka.karnataka.gov.in.
  • Register with personal and SHG details.
  • Select skill training or self-employment option.
  • Upload Aadhaar, caste certificate, and income proof.
  • Submit the application and follow updates via SMS or email.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Income & Caste Certificate (SC/ST/OBC if applicable)
  • Address Proof
  • Passport-size Photo
  • SHG/Training registration certificate (if available)

Additional Details

  • Implemented by the Department of Women and Child Development.
  • Some programs include entrepreneurship guidance and market linkages.
