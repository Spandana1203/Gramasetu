Karnataka Building & Other Construction Workers Scheme

Description: Implemented through the Karnataka BOCW Welfare Board, the scheme provides social security and welfare benefits to registered construction workers and their families. It covers financial aid, health benefits, pensions, scholarships, and insurance to protect workers from occupational hazards and improve living standards.

Eligibility

  • Must be a construction worker engaged in building or other construction work.
  • Permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Age between 18‚Äì60 years.
  • Registered with the Karnataka BOCW Welfare Board.
  • Not an income tax or GST payer.
  • Nominal registration and renewal fee paid.

Benefits

  • Financial assistance in case of accidents, disability, or death.
  • Medical expense reimbursement.
  • Pension for aged workers.
  • Scholarships for workers‚Äô children.
  • Maternity benefits for women workers.
  • Subsidies for tools, housing, and social welfare activities.
  • Funeral assistance in case of worker‚Äôs death.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card of worker.
  • Age proof (SSLC, birth certificate, or voter ID).
  • Residence proof (ration card, voter ID, electricity bill, etc.).
  • Proof of employment as a construction worker (employer certificate/affidavit).
  • Bank account details (Aadhaar-linked passbook).
  • Passport-size photographs.
  • Application form attested by employer/local authority.

How to Apply

  1. Visit Karnataka BOCW Welfare Board official website ‚Üí https://kbocwwb.karnataka.gov.in
  2. Download registration form or apply online.
  3. Fill personal, employment, and bank details.
  4. Attach all required documents and photographs.
  5. Submit to local Labour Dept./BOCW Welfare Board office.
  6. Pay one-time registration fee and annual renewal fee.
  7. Upon verification, welfare card issued to avail benefits.

Importance

  • Provides financial protection during accidents, illness, and old age.
  • Supports education for workers‚Äô children.
  • Reduces vulnerability of unorganized workers.
  • Enhances living standards and dignity of construction workers.
  • Encourages formal registration to bring workers into welfare system.
