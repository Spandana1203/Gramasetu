Karnataka Mathrushree Scheme ‚Äì Pregnant Women

Description: A maternity benefit program by Karnataka Government to improve maternal health and nutrition for pregnant and lactating women, providing financial assistance, promoting safe delivery, and institutional check-ups.

Eligibility

  • Pregnant woman and permanent resident of Karnataka.
  • Belonging to BPL families generally required.
  • Family income within prescribed limits.
  • Usually covers up to two live births per family.
  • Must not receive benefits under other maternity schemes.

Benefits

  • Monthly financial assistance (‚Çπ1,000/month for 3 months during pregnancy + 3 months after delivery, total ‚Çπ6,000).
  • Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to Aadhaar-linked bank account.
  • Encourages institutional deliveries in government or recognized hospitals.
  • Promotes regular antenatal check-ups and nutritional support.
  • Reduces maternal and infant mortality rates.
  • Provides economic relief to poor families.

Documents Required

  • Aadhaar card (bank-linked).
  • BPL ration card or income certificate.
  • Mother & Child Protection (MCP) card or ANC records.
  • Bank passbook copy (Aadhaar-seeded).
  • Residence proof (ration card/voter ID, etc.).
  • Passport-size photographs.

How to Apply

  1. Visit nearest Anganwadi center or Primary Health Centre (PHC) for pregnancy registration.
  2. Provide necessary details and documents to Anganwadi/ASHA worker.
  3. Fill out the application form (available via Seva Sindhu portal when active).
  4. Upload/submit documents in prescribed format.
  5. Verify Aadhaar-linked bank account for DBT.
  6. Track application status through Anganwadi workers or Seva Sindhu portal.

Importance

  • Financial aid during pregnancy and lactation.
  • Promotes regular health check-ups and institutional deliveries.
  • Supports better nutrition and reduces maternal anemia.
  • Enhances infant survival with safe postnatal care.
  • Strengthens families and communities, contributing to a healthier Karnataka.
